About us

Better When2Meet is a web app that helps you plan your next meeting. You can select the dates you want to met, set up your avaliability for these dates, and share it with other people so they can do the same. Then you just pick the time that works for everyone!

Privacy policy

We only store the necessary data. We never share it with anyone. No ads, no tracking, no unnecessary data collection. Email and password needed to save your events and give you the option to delete/edit them. Each event stores invited people to display their avaliability. That's all, folks.
